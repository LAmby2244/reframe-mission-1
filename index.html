What this is

Not a book.
A practice.

Rewrite Your Life is the Purposeful Change Reframe methodology — the same process that has helped nearly a million people across Microsoft, Nike, Shell, HSBC, and beyond — brought into your hands as a living workbook with an AI companion built to go deeper with you.

You don't read it. You do it. Then you come back and do it again, because the person reading it has changed.

Meet Lumen

A mirror,
not a coach

Lumen is an AI companion trained in the Reframe methodology and 25 years of Simon's coaching practice. It doesn't give you answers. It reads what you actually wrote — the word you chose, the tension, the absence — and asks the one question that takes you one layer deeper.

You decide when to go deeper. You decide when to stop. Lumen follows your lead.

"Lumen isn't here to fix you. It's a support, not a solution. A prompt for your own wisdom."

Lumen in action

About Simon

The squiggly
spiral

Simon Lamb has spent 25 years helping people and organisations change — not by telling them what to do, but by helping them see what they can't yet see about themselves.

He has taken this work into the rooms of Microsoft, Nike, Shell, HSBC, Co-op, and hundreds of others — not as a consultant but as a presence. Someone who gets on the stage without a script, holds the room, and makes it safe to be honest.

"Life is neither a straight line with a destination, nor a loop where we never change. It's a squiggly spiral — where even the collapses are part of the evolution."

Rewrite Your Life is 25 years of that work, decoded and made available to everyone. Written with Christina Watt, CPCC ACC, and built as a living product — not a PDF, not a course, but a practice you can return to.
