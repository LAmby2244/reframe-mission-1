How missions work ▼

Each mission is a small, self-organised personal development project. Read the chapter first, then do the mission. They build on each other — each one begins with a Bridge connecting where you just came from to where you are going next, so the whole arc builds on itself rather than starting fresh each time.

01
Experiment
Notice your stories and self-talk in the moments that matter. Test things in the real world — this is where new wiring begins.
02
Reflect
Look for patterns in what repeats and what that might reveal. Insight that isn't named stays invisible.
03
Share
Bring it into conversation. Let someone else's presence deepen your insight. Saying it out loud changes something.
04
What next
Choose one small shift and stay with it until it begins to take root. Change doesn't come from thinking differently — it comes from being different.
