Privacy Policy

Effective date: 7 April 2026 · Last updated: 7 April 2026
Purposeful Change Ltd · United Kingdom

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we store and protect it, and your rights over it. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

1. Who we are

This privacy policy applies to the Reframe platform and Body Signal tool, operated by Purposeful Change Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). Our products include the Reframe digital workbook, the Body Signal wearable data integration, and the Lumen AI companion.

For questions about this policy, contact us at hello@purposefulchange.com.

2. What data we collect

Account data: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password. This is used to authenticate you and associate your reflections with your account.

Wearable biometric data: If you connect a WHOOP device, we access the following data from the WHOOP API with your explicit authorisation:

  • Recovery score and recovery cycle data
  • Heart rate variability (HRV) — rMSSD
  • Resting heart rate (RHR)
  • Sleep performance, sleep duration, sleep need, and sleep stress
  • Day strain and workout activity logs
  • Non-activity stress and total day stress
  • Respiratory rate

This data is used solely to generate your Body Signal score and trigger the appropriate psychological reflection flow. It is stored in our secure database associated with your account.

Reflection entries: When you complete a Body Signal exploration, we store your written answers to the reflection questions, the signal state that triggered the exploration, the WHOOP data snapshot for that day, and the Lumen AI response.

Lumen AI conversation data: Your conversations with Lumen are stored to enable pattern recognition across sessions — this is central to how Lumen identifies recurring patterns over time. Lumen's responses are generated by Claude (Anthropic). We do not use your conversation data to train any AI model.

Usage data: We collect standard server logs including page views, API calls, and error events. These are used for debugging and platform improvement only.

3. Why we collect this data

We collect and process your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide the service: Biometric data is required to generate your daily signal state and Exploration Score. Reflection entries are required for Lumen to read patterns across time.
  • To conduct research: With your consent, anonymised and aggregated data from your participation may be used in our longitudinal research study examining the relationship between psychological pattern work and physiological recovery. This research will never identify you individually. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • To improve the platform: Usage data helps us understand how the product is being used and where it can be improved.

4. Research participation

The Body Signal platform is currently running a 90-day longitudinal study examining whether structured psychological pattern work — initiated by biometric signals — correlates with improvements in physiological recovery over time. If you are participating in this study, your data (biometric trends, reflection entry frequency, Lumen-identified pattern tags, and Pattern Recurrence Rate) will be analysed as part of this research.

All research analysis uses anonymised data. No individually identifying information is included in research outputs. You may withdraw from the study at any time by contacting us — your data will be deleted from the research dataset within 30 days.

5. How we store and protect your data

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database service with servers located in the European Union. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access to your data is protected by Row Level Security — meaning each user can only access their own data, and no other user or staff member can read your reflections or biometric data without your explicit consent.

WHOOP OAuth tokens (which allow us to read your WHOOP data on your behalf) are stored securely and are never exposed to the client. Tokens are refreshed automatically and can be revoked by you at any time through your WHOOP account settings or by contacting us.

Lumen AI responses are generated via the Anthropic API. Conversation data sent to Anthropic is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. We use Anthropic's API with zero data retention settings where available.

6. Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly for the purpose of delivering the service:

  • Supabase — database storage and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI responses via the Claude API (Lumen)
  • Vercel — platform hosting and API routing
  • WHOOP — biometric data source (you authorise this connection directly)

Each provider processes your data only as necessary to provide their service and is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and applicable data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion: You can request that all your data be deleted. We will action this within 30 days.
  • Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent: You can disconnect your WHOOP account at any time. This stops any further biometric data collection. Previously collected data will be retained unless you request deletion.
  • Object to research use: You can withdraw from the research study at any time without affecting your use of the platform.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@purposefulchange.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data retention

We retain your account data and reflection entries for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days. WHOOP biometric data is retained for the duration of your account to enable longitudinal pattern analysis — this is core to the product's value. You may request deletion of specific data types at any time.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required as we use only strictly necessary cookies.

10. Children

The Reframe platform and Body Signal tool are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account immediately.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as the platform develops. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated. Continued use of the platform after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact and complaints

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at hello@purposefulchange.com.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the United Kingdom: ico.org.uk.
