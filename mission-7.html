Feedback is a Gift

The patterns you've been uncovering in yourself are patterns other people have already noticed. This mission gives you a structured way to hear them - not as criticism, but as data.

Where you're coming from

In Mission 6 you built your trigger map. You tracked the moments of activation, looked for the patterns, and began to understand what is really driving your responses. You practised pressing pause.

Now the work turns outward. Everything you have been discovering about yourself - the patterns, the values, the triggers, the stories - other people have already been noticing. We try hard to hide things from the outer world and fail, because we leak. This mission gives you a structured way to hear them. Not as criticism. As data.
